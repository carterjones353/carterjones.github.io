Poor Dan is in a Droop

Digital Interactive Comic

May 2023

Poor Dan is in a Droop is a semester-long collaboration between 5 people, having 3 main artists and 2 main developers. We were assigned an unproven topic within quantum physics to attempt to convey through interactivity and visuals. This comic portrays the bi-directional flow of time through the life story of Dan, and his mental-health struggles.
